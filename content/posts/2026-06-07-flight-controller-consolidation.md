The flight controller is the quiet nervous system of any drone, and it is steadily absorbing functions that once lived on separate boards. The result is fewer components doing more.

## Technical Breakdown

- **Integration:** navigation, sensor fusion, and increasingly autonomy hooks land on a single controller.
- **Weight / wiring:** consolidation removes connectors and harnesses — a direct reliability and endurance benefit.
- **Flight controller:** higher onboard compute headroom leaves room for future capability via firmware.

## Industry Impact

For **manufacturers**, a more capable controller platform shortens the path to new features. For **operators**, fewer parts means fewer failure points and simpler maintenance. For **integrators**, standardised, capable controllers make it easier to build on top. Avionics consolidation is one of those unglamorous shifts that quietly raises the whole platform's ceiling.

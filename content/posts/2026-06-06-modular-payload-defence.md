Defence procurement is learning the same lesson the commercial world did: a platform that can change roles by changing payloads beats a fleet of single-purpose airframes. Modularity is becoming the organising principle.

## Technical Breakdown

- **Platform:** common airframe and avionics with standardised payload interfaces.
- **Payload / sensors:** ISR, communications relay, and other modules swap without redesigning the aircraft. Specific mission-system details are kept high level. **[Redacted for safety]**
- **Autonomy level:** shared autonomy stacks carry across payload configurations.

## Industry Impact

For **manufacturers**, the moat shifts toward the interface standard and the autonomy stack rather than any single airframe. For **operators**, modularity means faster reconfiguration and longer platform life. For **regulators and procurement**, common architectures simplify certification and sustainment. The direction of travel favours flexible fleets over bespoke aircraft.
